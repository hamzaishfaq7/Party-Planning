About Tanzy's Event Planning

Tanzy's Event Planning is an boutique wedding and events planning company serving in Toronto, the GTA, and beyond. Known for planning, designing, and coordinating timeless events with a contemporary, free-spirited twist, I am a true believer that it's the one-of-a-kind details that will make your event unique to you! With a love for understated elegance, timeless design, and modern, contemporary details, Tanzy's Event Planning + Design will help you create an unforgettable, everyone's-still-talking-about-it-five-years-later kind of celebration.
